Training & Certifications

All Clean Globe Environmental (CGE) field personnel are trained to safely perform the work that they do. Environmental Technicians, Equipment Operators, and Laborers are taught to recognize hazards and how to deal with them. CGE personnel have the following training / certifications:

  • • 40 hour OSHA HAZWOPER,
  • • 8 hour OSHA Supervisor's,
  • • 10 hour OSHA Construction Industry,
  • • American Petroleum Institute (API) Safe Work,
  • • Life Saving Rules for Shell Oil US
  • • Loss Prevention System (LPS) for ExxonMobil
  • • Control of Work (COW) for BP
  • • American Red Cross First Aid / CPR, and
  • • US Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)
